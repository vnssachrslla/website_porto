About Able Ballet

Able ballet is a non-profit Foundation of ballet based in Melbourne City.
Since 2017, we have held performance at some small theatres in Melbourne. We also held some perform tour to few large provincial towns in Victoria such as Geelong and Ballarat. We are aiming in promoting the exellence in ballet and conteporary dance by enabling an exchange between local and international dance artists and scholars.

Our Goals

We want to bring up the importance of contemporary arts in terms of ballet dance. We also want to be a world class platform of teenagers and adult (in between 14-25 year old) ballerina to express their talent and grow more their interest in arts.

Sophia Isabelle was born in Sydney in 1992. She has her passion in ballet since she was 5 years old when her mom decided to make her attend a ballet class for kids. She has spent her entire life dedicated to ballet and has been coaching a lot of ballet group across the world. She is one of the most high-certified ballerina coach in Australia. In 2017, she decided to establish Able Ballet.

Anna Marie was born in Russia, in 1992. She studied ballet since she was 6 years old in Imperial Ballet School. She has been into many historic tour of Russian Ballerina since she was 15 years old. In 2016 she moved to Melbourne and started teaching primary-school kids in a ballet academy.

Jacqueline is originally from Malaysia, was born in 1996. She is our youngest main member however, she is incridibly fast learners and has great history in ballet world. She has won a lot of world-class competition as a duo ballerina. Her partner went back to Italy meanwhile she decided to move to Melbourne in 2015. She was also the co-founder of Able Ballet.
